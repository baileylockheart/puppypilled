Okay, so it's been QUITE a while since my last page here, but I wanted to give an update about something! In my last post, I talked about how I got a dog tag, and while I liked having that, I wanted more. It bothered me a bit, having it as a keychain and nothing else. So, I got a collar to go with it!!

It's cotton and hemp, so it's super soft, and it's really comfortable! It's like a choker necklace, but I like it a lot more than my old one! Having the buckle in the front is nice, it's much less annoying than the snap thing that my choker had. There was nothing wrong with the old one, it's just that this is better!

I've been wearing this pretty much every day since I got it, and I like it a lot! I used to wear a different necklace every day, and while it feels weird not wearing it all the time, this one's a lot nicer sensory-wise?? And somehow, it makes me less dysphoric! Normal necklaces never made me particularly dysphoric in the first place, and I still like them, but this just feels better, somehow.

Soooo, yeah! I don't really have much else to say, honestly! I'm really happy with the collar situation, and I'm hoping to get a pair of ears soon! If/when that happens, I'll definitely come and talk about it here! See you next time!
